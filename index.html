About Me

Hello! My name is Jolie. I am an aspiring 3D artist and designer at Ngee Ann Polytechnic studying to graduate with a Diploma in Immersive Media. I love to play online games with my friends and draw. I also enjoy watching anime and cartoons. My interest in design started in secondary school when my friend would often draw characters from shows we watched. Soon after I joined the Art Club so I would be able to be exposed to more art content. Along the years, our teacher got us to participate in various art compeitions Afterwards, I was more keen into learning design, which was what brought me into the Immersive Media course.

Live On Design Compeition 2019
xx x 2019
Poster design for a competition aimed to promote organ donation awareness.

The girl in the art piece has passed away due to some unfortunate events. She died peacefully, knowing that her organs would be donated to other people who need it. As seen in the photographs surrounding her, her organs saved the lives of a few people. After recovering, these people went on to do the things they were not able to when they were hospitalised or sick. They were thankful to the girl who gave them a second chance in life, hence, the photographs have the words ‘thank you’ written on them, around her.
